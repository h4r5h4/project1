Why Student Portal ?

A student portal is an online gateway where students can log into a school website to access their marks, attendance, e-books and other important information. Using a portal makes it easier for people to access important information from anywhere, at any time of day. The portals are being commonly used in colleges and universities where prompt information and necessary updates must be readily available to a large number of students.
